Game Logic How to Play

Municipality

  • You collect waste from Residential, Commercial, and Industrial districts.
  • You balance city metrics: budget, city health, and carbon emissions.
  • You complete city projects using Grade A/B materials to boost city health.
TAKEN

MRF

  • You process and sort waste into usable materials (A/B/C grades).
  • You run Sorting, Recycling, and Waste Treatment to reduce low-grade waste.
  • You decide what to send to Broker while controlling processing costs and impact.
TAKEN

Broker

  • You move materials and waste between Municipality and MRF.
  • You manage transport choices (truck/port/airport), speed, capacity, and cost.
  • You trade in the marketplace to support team flow and avoid bottlenecks.
